Community Objectives

Help the community understand who you are and what you stand for. These statements form the heart of your Community Page — the more thoughtful and complete, the stronger your page will be.

A strong universal vision adds credibility and inspires wider support.

Financial Statements

Financial transparency is central to this platform. Upload your financial statements using our CSV template so your Community Page can display a clear picture of your income and expenses.

Incomplete financials are one of the most common reasons submissions are not approved. Even estimates are acceptable — clarity and honesty matter more than perfection.
⬇️ Download Template Must include at least one income row and one expense row. Use the section column to mark each row as income or expense. The notes column is optional but recommended for context.

Budget

Your budget shows your community how resources are being planned and managed. Purchased items show accountability. Expected items show planning. Desired items show ambition and invite support.

Including desired items is optional, but communities that share their wish list often attract more targeted donations and in-kind contributions.
⬇️ Download Template Must include at least one purchased or expected item and a contingency row. Use purchased, expected, desired, or contingency in the status column.

Donation & Payment

Let your community know how to support you financially. This information is displayed publicly on your Community Page. Include every method you actively accept — more options means more accessibility for your supporters.

Submissions without at least one active donation method cannot be approved. Including a transparency statement about how donations are used significantly increases donor confidence.
⬇️ Download Template Must include at least one active method and its handle or address. Accepted methods: PayPal, Stripe, Postal, POS, Manual, Cryptocurrency.
Strong transparency statements build long-term donor trust.
